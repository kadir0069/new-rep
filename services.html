Diagnostic Services

We offer a wide range of diagnostic services to accurately assess your health needs.

Blood Tests

Comprehensive blood analysis including complete blood count (CBC), lipid profile, glucose levels, liver function tests, and more.

  • Fasting required for some tests
  • Results in 24-48 hours
  • Home collection available
Book Now

Ultrasound

Non-invasive imaging using sound waves to examine internal organs, blood flow, and developing fetuses during pregnancy.

  • Abdominal and pelvic scans
  • Doppler studies for blood flow
  • Obstetric ultrasound
Book Now

X-Ray

Digital X-ray imaging for bones, chest, abdomen, and other areas with minimal radiation exposure and quick results.

  • Digital imaging for better clarity
  • Low radiation dose
  • Immediate results for emergencies
Book Now

MRI Scan

High-resolution magnetic resonance imaging for detailed examination of organs, tissues, and skeletal system without radiation.

  • 3 Tesla machine for high resolution
  • Contrast and non-contrast options
  • Specialized cardiac and neuro MRI
Book Now

ECG

Electrocardiogram tests to monitor heart health, detect arrhythmias, and assess overall cardiac function.

  • Resting and stress ECG
  • Holter monitoring available
  • Immediate interpretation
Book Now

CT Scan

Computed tomography scans providing cross-sectional images of the body for detailed diagnostic information.

  • 64-slice CT scanner
  • Low-dose protocols available
  • Angiography and 3D reconstruction
Book Now
