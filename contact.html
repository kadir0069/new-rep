Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and procedures.

Preparation depends on the specific test you're having. For blood tests, fasting for 8-12 hours may be required. For imaging tests like MRI or CT scans, you may need to avoid certain foods or medications. We provide detailed preparation instructions when you book your appointment.

Turnaround times vary by test. Routine blood tests are typically available within 24-48 hours. More complex tests like biopsies may take 3-5 days. Imaging results are usually ready within 24 hours for urgent cases and 48-72 hours for routine scans. We'll inform you of the expected timeline when you have your test.

Some tests require a doctor's referral, while others can be done as self-requested. Generally, imaging tests like MRI, CT scans, and specialized blood tests need a referral. Basic blood tests and some screenings can be done without one. Contact us to check requirements for your specific test.

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare and Medicaid. Please bring your insurance card and any required referral forms to your appointment. For specific coverage questions, contact our billing department at billing@smarthospital.com or call +8801815-505050 .

Yes, we offer a secure patient portal where you can access your test results, medical history, and appointment details. You'll receive login credentials when you register with us. For immediate assistance with the portal, email portal@smarthospital.com or call our support line.
